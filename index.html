About Max

Max Emilian Verstappen, born September 30, 1997, in Hasselt, Belgium, is a Dutch racing driver competing in Formula One for Red Bull Racing. He made history as the youngest driver to start a Formula One race at 17 years old. Max clinched his first Grand Prix victory in 2016 at the Spanish Grand Prix, becoming the youngest winner in F1 history at that time. Since then, he has amassed multiple World Championships, showcasing his relentless drive and exceptional skill on the track.

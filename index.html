About Max

Max Emilian Verstappen is a Dutch racing driver currently competing in Formula One with Red Bull Racing. Known for his aggressive style and brilliant overtakes, Max became the youngest driver to start a Formula One race and has since broken numerous records, including becoming a multi-time World Champion.
